Liquidity pools are the source of what we buy and sell on decentralized exchanges like raydium or Jupiter. Anyone can add their assets to an LP in exchange for rewards. You can earn small percentages for keeping your tokens in an LP but you need to understand the concept of impermanent loss. Basically if you are providing liquidity for a very volatile asset you may end up with less than what you put in depending on when you decide to remove your liquidity. It may have been better for you to simply hold the tokens. It’s up to each person if you want to contribute to lps or not. If you have assets that are just lying around and you want to earn some passive income, it could be a good option. In order to add to an LP though you will need both tokens of the pair.
